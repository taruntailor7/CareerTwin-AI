01 · The Problem

Career advice today is generic, expensive, or nonexistent

Everyone has a resume. Almost nobody has a career strategist. The tools that exist tell you where you've been — none of them tell you how ready you are, or what happens next.

🧭

No personalized direction

Job boards and LinkedIn show what roles exist — not whether you're actually ready for them, or how to close the gap.

💸

Real coaching is expensive

A human career coach costs hundreds per session. Most students and early professionals never get that feedback loop.

🎲

Decisions made on guesswork

Switching roles, asking for a promotion, or pivoting industries is a leap of faith — there's no way to simulate the outcome first.

02 · The Solution

A living digital twin of your professional self

CareerTwin AI imports what already exists about you, builds an AI-scored profile, and simulates real career decisions — before you make them.

📥

Import, don't type

Upload a resume or paste LinkedIn/GitHub/portfolio URLs — AI extracts and fills your profile automatically.

🎯

Career Scorecard

Recruiter-grade scoring across resume, ATS, LinkedIn, GitHub, and portfolio — each with a "why" and a fix.

🔮

Career Simulation

Model any career move in plain English and get success probability, salary projection, and skill gaps.

📄

Shareable Report

A polished, visual PDF report you can hand to a mentor, manager, or keep for yourself.

03 · What We Built

A complete, end-to-end product — not a mockup

Every feature below is live and working in the deployed product today.

🧬

Adaptive onboarding

Tailored fields, reflection questions, and AI interview for students, working professionals, and people between jobs.

🤖

Real AI interview

Gemini-powered conversation that adapts to the profile, feeding richer context into the Twin.

📊

Interactive dashboard

Career Twin summary, scorecards, radar charts, and per-source analysis (resume, LinkedIn, GitHub, portfolio).

🧪

Custom simulations

Type any scenario in natural language and get a modeled outcome — not a fixed, hardcoded number.

🗂️

Multi-profile support

Create and switch between multiple Career Twins per account (e.g. different career paths).

📑

Rich PDF reports

Dashboard-quality visual reports — score rings, bars, and action plans — downloadable by name.
